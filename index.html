Questions People Ask Before Booking

How are your prices so much lower than stores?

Low overhead + by-appointment model + direct sourcing = 50-80% off retail. No big showroom lease, no commissioned staff, no retail markup. Those savings go straight to you.

Do I really need an appointment?

Yes — and that’s actually the advantage. You get the showroom to yourself, Tre’s full attention, and a private shopping experience. Same-day appointments are often available. Just call or book online.

Can I actually take a mattress home today?

Most of the time, yes. Many mattresses are compressed in a box and ready for same-day pickup. Inventory changes weekly so book your appointment and Tre will confirm what’s available.

What if I have bad credit or no credit?

Ask about Snap Finance and other no-credit-needed options. Most customers are approved quickly. Don’t assume no — come in and ask Tre.

What brands do you carry?

Brooklyn Bedding, Diamond Mattress, Lunessence, and Malouf — all at warehouse pricing. Brand and model availability varies weekly. Book an appointment to check current inventory.

What sizes do you have?

Twin from $130, Full from $140, Queen from $150, King from $275. Plus foundations, frames, and accessories depending on inventory.

Do you deliver?

Most mattresses are boxed for self-pickup — which saves you the delivery fee. Ask Tre about delivery options if you need them.

What about warranty and returns?

All mattresses come with manufacturer warranties. Tre will walk you through the details before you commit — no surprises.
