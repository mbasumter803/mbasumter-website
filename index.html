Questions People Ask Before Booking

How are your prices so much lower than stores?

Low overhead + by-appointment model + direct sourcing = 50-80% off retail. No big showroom lease, no commissioned staff, no retail markup. Those savings go straight to you.

Do I really need an appointment?

Yes — and that’s actually the advantage. You get the showroom to yourself, Tre’s full attention, and a private shopping experience. Same-day appointments are often available. Just call or book online.

Can I actually take a mattress home today?

Most of the time, yes. Many mattresses are compressed in a box and ready for same-day pickup. Inventory changes weekly so book your appointment and Tre will confirm what’s available.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — through Snap Finance. No credit check required, flexible payment terms, and most customers are approved in minutes. $5 down can get you sleeping on a name-brand mattress tonight. Tre will walk you through it when you come in — no pressure, no surprises.
