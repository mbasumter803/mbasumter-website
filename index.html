Common Questions

How is your pricing so low?

We operate by appointment only out of a private warehouse — no big retail overhead, no commissioned salespeople, no fancy showroom leases. Those savings go straight to you: 50-80% off what the same mattress costs at a chain store.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — we're by appointment only. That's how we keep prices low and give you full attention. Call 803-795-1194 or book online in 60 seconds. Same-day appointments usually available.

Do you deliver?

No delivery needed! Every mattress we carry is mattress-in-a-box — compressed and boxed so it fits in any car, SUV, or truck. Take it home the same day you buy it. Saves you time and money.

What brands do you carry?

Malouf, Serta, Sealy, Simmons, Southerland, and Allswell — all premium, name-brand mattresses at warehouse pricing.

What if I have bad credit?

No problem. We offer Snap Finance with fast approval and flexible payments — no credit needed. Most customers are approved in minutes. Everyone gets a shot.

Do you carry boxsprings?

Today's mattresses are designed to work without boxsprings — they sit right on a platform bed, slatted frame, or adjustable base. Simpler setup, less money spent, same great sleep.

What about returns and warranty?

All our mattresses come with full manufacturer warranties (most are 10-year). We'll walk you through warranty details at your appointment.

How long does a mattress-in-a-box take to expand?

Most mattresses fully expand within a few hours of unboxing. For best results, unroll it on your bed frame, let it breathe, and you can sleep on it the same night.
