Common Questions

How is your pricing so low?

We use a low-overhead, by-appointment model and warehouse-style pricing, which helps customers save 50–80% compared to traditional retail stores. No big showroom lease, no commissioned salespeople — those savings go to you.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. Appointments allow us to give you a private shopping experience and the showroom to yourself. Same-day appointments are often available. Call 803-795-1194 or book online.

Can I take a mattress home today?

Many mattress-in-a-box options are available for same-day pickup depending on current inventory. Book your appointment and Tre will confirm what's available.

Do you deliver?

Same-day pickup is available on many items — most mattresses are compressed in a box and fit in your vehicle. Ask Tre about delivery options if needed.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. Ask about flexible financing options, including no-credit-needed options through Snap Finance. Approval, terms, and availability may vary.

Do you carry box springs or foundations?

Yes, availability varies by inventory. Ask Tre during your appointment about foundations, platform frames, and accessories.

What sizes do you carry?

Twin, Full, Queen, King, and more depending on current inventory. Inventory changes weekly — book today to check what's available.

What about returns and warranty?

Warranty and return details vary by product. Tre will explain all the details before you buy so you know exactly what you're getting.

How long does a mattress-in-a-box take to expand?

Most begin expanding quickly after opening, but full expansion can vary by mattress. Most are comfortable to sleep on the same night.
